Face Mask Detection System
-Using Deep Learning

Detailed Explanation

Demo Video

Face mask detection system is a Deep learning model designed to identify whether a person is wearing a face mask or not. Since, COVID-19 has seriously threatened the safety of human life, and quickly spread to the majority of countries worldwide, WHO has recommended everyone to wear a face mask in public places to stop the spread of COVID-19.


To design this model, we have used deep learning algorithms – CNNs (Convolution Neural Network) and RNNs (Recurrent Neural Network). In case of CNN, simple 2D convolution architecture and MobileNetsV2 architecture have been used to design two different models based on the same problem statement. In case of RNN, LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) and GRU (Gated Recurrent Unit) architecture has been used to design two models so that we can compare the final results and accuracy among all the models.


Features:

  • ✅This model is capable of scanning and detecting "face mask" in realtime.
  • ✅This model is also capable of detecting multiple faces at the same time.
  • ✅It can detect easily whether a person is wearing face mask or not.

Tools and Technologies used

  • ▶️Programming Language used: Python
  • ▶️Deep Learning architecture used:
    • ➡️CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) architectures: (a)Simple 2D convolution (b) MobileNets V2
    • ➡️RNN (Recurrent Neural Network) architectures: (a)LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) (b) GRU (Gated Recurrent Unit)
  • ▶️Tensorflow
  • ▶️OpenCV
